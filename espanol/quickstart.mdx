### Set up your account

If you haven't already, register to get an X-Mart account and get started. You can create an account [in our dashboard](https://app.x-mart.io/auth/signin).

### How to set up
<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="message-bot" title="Send your menu synchronizations">
    You can send your menu synchronizations taking into account [the necessary configuration](https://docs.x-mart.io/api-reference/authentication/login/post) provided by X-Mart. Make sure to follow all the steps and specific settings to ensure proper integration.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
