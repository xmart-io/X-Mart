## What is X-Mart?

X-Mart is a comprehensive suite of services designed to optimize data management and processing across various business applications. Our system enables efficient synchronization of menus sent by clients, facilitating seamless and accurate integration. Additionally, X-Mart offers a web service for order injection, ensuring effective and real-time communication with our clients' systems. We also manage the processes for order status changes, ensuring every operation is conducted in an organized and controlled manner.

With X-Mart, businesses can rely on a robust and flexible platform capable of adapting to their operational needs and significantly enhancing process efficiency.

## Main Features

Delivery:

This microservice specializes in the synchronization, processing, and storage of our clients' menus in our database. By implementing advanced data integration and management techniques, Admin ensures efficient and accurate menu updates, enabling optimized real-time information management.

Pickup:

This microservice is dedicated to generating payment requests towards payment buttons. This service manages and coordinates payment requests efficiently and securely, ensuring the proper execution of financial transactions within our platform. Thanks to its robust architecture, it optimizes the flow of payments and facilitates integration with various payment systems.

Kioskos:

This microservice specializes in managing state changes and complements the notification service, allowing customers to receive real-time updates on the status of their orders. Thanks to its efficient integration, it ensures smooth and accurate communication, enhancing the end-user experience and optimizing order tracking.
