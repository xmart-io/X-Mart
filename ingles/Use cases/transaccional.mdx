## Key Features:

- **High Scalability:**
Ability to process a large volume of transactions per second, ensuring consistent performance even during high-demand periods.

- **Guaranteed Availability:**
Designed to operate with highly available uptime.

**Notable Example:** On Children's Day, June 1, 2024, the order service demonstrated its scalability by processing over **11,000 transactions** 
per hour multiple times throughout the day. Despite the high demand, response times remained low, ensuring a seamless experience for users.

## Common Use Cases:
**1.** Bulk order processing during seasonal marketing campaigns, such as Children's Day or special promotions.  

**2.** Concurrent order management across different geographic regions. 

**3.** Integration with external systems for order tracking and fulfillment.
